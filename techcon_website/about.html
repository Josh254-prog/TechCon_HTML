Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small gathering of local tech enthusiasts in a university auditorium. Its initial aim was to foster knowledge sharing and collaboration within the nascent tech community. Over the years, it steadily grew, attracting speakers and attendees from across the region, and by 2015, it had become a nationally recognized event. The conference has consistently evolved with the industry, incorporating new tracks on AI, blockchain, cybersecurity, and sustainable tech, solidifying its reputation as a forward-thinking platform for technological discourse and innovation.

Our Mission

Our mission at TechCon is to empower the global technology community by providing an unparalleled platform for learning, networking, and innovation. We strive to connect aspiring talent with industry veterans, facilitate the exchange of groundbreaking ideas, and inspire the next generation of tech leaders. We are committed to fostering an inclusive environment where diverse perspectives are celebrated, and collaborative solutions to the world's most pressing technological challenges are forged.
